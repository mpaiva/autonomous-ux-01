The Growth Lead owns the question that determines whether the company lives: will AI startups pay for this, and how do we reach them? This role runs the content/IP-led go-to-market, the paid AI-UX Audit wedge, founder-led outbound through investor networks, and the pricing experiments that turn our hypotheses into a real rate card.

**Why this role exists now.** Demand risk is the single risk that can kill the company. It deserves a dedicated owner from day one, not a "we'll figure out sales later" posture.

**What success looks like.** A repeatable path from public IP → audit → embedded retainer; validated pricing; a pipeline of seed–Series A AI startups; clients who renew and refer.
