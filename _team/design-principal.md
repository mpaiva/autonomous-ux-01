The Design Principal owns the thing clients actually pay for: the quality of the work. This role defines what "good" means for AI-product design at Throughline, builds the reusable AI-UX pattern library that becomes both IP and marketing, and makes sure agent-augmented production still clears a senior bar.

**Why this role exists now.** Our entire differentiation rests on craft that is specifically good at the hard AI-UX problems — agent supervision, trust, onboarding. Without an owner of that standard, "AI-native" is just a tagline.

**What success looks like.** Clients renew because the work is visibly better than what freelancers or legacy agencies produce; the pattern library compounds into a defensible asset; AI tooling raises output without lowering the bar.
