## Context

We need price points to start selling, but we have no signed clients yet. Market research gave us real anchors; we turn those into a starting hypothesis rather than a committed rate card.

## Decision (status: proposed — pending validation)

| Offer | Indicative price | Anchor from research |
|---|---|---|
| AI UX Audit (wedge) | **$6–12k fixed** | Focused sprints start ~$5k; high-signal scoping |
| Embedded AI design partner | **$10–18k / mo** | Above generic fractional ($3–10k/mo), below large embedded teams ($12–30k/mo); justified by scarce agent/LLM-UX expertise |
| AI UX systems (build) | **$20–60k / project** | Typical agency projects $15–50k; full builds $100k+ |
| Equity | **1–3% selective upside** | Designer-equity norms; stay cash-positive |

## Rationale

Research showed a proven productized band (~$5–8k/mo subscriptions like DesignJoy/Superside) and a fractional band ($3–10k/mo), with **no public price card** for AI-specialized fractional leads — a gap we can price into from above on expertise scarcity. We anchor in proven bands and add a specialization premium. ([Research]({{ '/research/0001-design-studio-pricing/' | relative_url }}))

## Assumptions & risks

- **Status is `proposed`, not `decided`**, precisely because willingness-to-pay evidence is thin (flagged in research). The first 5 ICP conversations and 1–2 sold audits will confirm or move these numbers.
- **Assumption:** specialization commands a premium rather than narrowing the buyer pool too far.
