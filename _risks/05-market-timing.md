## The risk

Our ICP's ability to pay is tied to AI startup funding. A downturn in AI venture funding would shrink budgets exactly where we sell, compressing demand and pricing simultaneously.

## Mitigation

- Anchor offerings to **revenue-linked triggers** (enterprise pilots, churn, fundraising) that stay urgent even in a downturn — trust UX that unblocks a deal is ROI-positive regardless of market mood.
- Keep the cost base low so we can weather a softer market.
- The fixed-fee audit remains affordable even when retainer budgets tighten.

## Open questions

- How correlated is design spend with the broader funding cycle for our specific ICP?
