## The risk

Early on, the four founding functions are held by very few people plus the agent fleet. Client delivery, GTM, and IP creation compete for the same scarce hours; a single point of failure (illness, churn, overload) stalls the company.

## Mitigation

- **Agent leverage** is the primary buffer — productized playbooks let a small team deliver more without proportional headcount.
- Sequence carefully: validate demand before taking on delivery volume that outruns capacity.
- Stage hiring against revenue (additional designers first, then Finance/Ops, then Recruiting).

## Open questions

- At what revenue does the first additional human designer become necessary vs. nice-to-have?
- Which functions are safe to keep agent-heavy longest?
