# Outreach kit

> Public artifact: ICP criteria, sourcing channels, and message templates. The list of **named** candidate companies and people is kept in an internal working doc (not this public repo) for privacy ([R-06]({{ '/risks/06-confidentiality/' | relative_url }})).

## ICP screen (a prospect must clear all four)

1. **Stage:** seed–Series A (roughly $2–20M raised, ~10–50 people).
2. **Product:** applied AI shipping to real users — agentic, copilot, or vertical-AI SaaS. *Not* a foundation-model lab, *not* pre-seed, *not* a large incumbent.
3. **Surface area:** has agent/automation/HITL features, or sells into enterprises (where trust UX bites).
4. **Reachability:** a founder/product leader we can reach via a warm intro or a credible public channel.

## Sourcing channels

- Warm intros from the **Chairman's** investor/operator network (highest conversion — see open question in [P-0003]({{ '/log/0003-start-icp-conversations/' | relative_url }})).
- Recent **YC / accelerator** AI batches; named **VC AI portfolios**.
- **Product Hunt / Show HN / X** launches of AI agents and copilots in the last 6–12 months.
- AI-engineering and applied-AI **communities and conferences**.

## Message templates

**These are research-recruiting messages, not sales.** The ask is 25 minutes to learn, never a pitch. Keep them short and specific to the recipient's product.

### A. Warm intro (via a mutual contact)
> Hi [name] — [mutual] thought we should connect. I'm researching how seed/Series-A AI teams handle the trust and oversight side of their product UX (agent supervision, onboarding, enterprise trust). Not selling anything — I'm trying to learn what's actually painful. Could I borrow 25 min to hear how you've handled it at [company]? Happy to share what I'm learning across teams in return.

### B. Cold (LinkedIn / X / email)
> Hi [name] — I'm doing founder research on AI-product UX, specifically how teams like [company] handle [a specific trust/agent/onboarding detail visible in their product]. I'm not pitching — just trying to understand what's real. Would you trade 25 min for a synthesis of what I hear across ~10 AI teams? No worries if not.

### C. Async written (when a call won't fit)
> Totally fine to do this in writing. Three quick ones: (1) Last time a user didn't trust or got confused by [product] — what happened and did it cost a deal? (2) Where does product experience rank against model quality and GTM right now? (3) Last time you paid an outside firm for design/product work — what triggered it? Reply whenever; I'll send back the cross-team synthesis.

## Guardrails

- No incentives; this is peer research, which keeps signal honest.
- Personalize the bracketed detail from the recipient's real product — generic blasts get ignored and corrupt the signal.
- Capture every response in the [interview log]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/log/' | relative_url }}). Keep client-identifying detail **out** of the public record.
- Target **5 completed** conversations; expect to contact ~15–25 prospects to get there.
