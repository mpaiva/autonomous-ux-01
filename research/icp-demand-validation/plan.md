# ICP demand-validation sprint — plan

**Purpose:** Directly attack [R-01 — will AI startups actually pay for design?]({{ '/risks/01-demand/' | relative_url }}), the one risk that can kill the company, and move [pricing (D-0006)]({{ '/decisions/0006-pricing-hypothesis/' | relative_url }}) from `proposed` toward `decided`.

> **Honest status: apparatus ready, 0 of 5 conversations conducted. Demand remains UNVALIDATED.** Building this kit does not reduce R-01. The risk moves only when real founders have spoken and the signals below are met.

## What "started" means here

The founding team's agents cannot be a human voice in a real founder conversation, and we will **not** manufacture conversations or quotes. "Starting" the conversations decomposes into:

1. **Design the discovery** — guide, hypotheses, confirm/kill signals, capture template. ✅ Done (team work).
2. **Source real ICP founders** — candidate companies sourced (internal working list); reaching real people needs the Chairman's intros or authorization to do outreach. ⏳ Pending Chairman ([P-0003]({{ '/log/0003-start-icp-conversations/' | relative_url }})).
3. **Conduct + synthesize** — human-run conversations captured in the [interview log]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/log/' | relative_url }}), then synthesized into a research entry. ⏳ Not started (0/5).

## Hypotheses

- **H1 (problem):** AI-product UX (esp. agent supervision / trust) is a top-3 felt problem blocking retention or enterprise deals.
- **H2 (trigger):** Spend is triggered by a stalled enterprise pilot, a fundraise, or churn.
- **H3 (wedge):** A fixed-fee 1–2 week AI UX Audit is a low-enough-risk first step to win a real commitment.
- **H4 (pricing):** The audit sits in a $6–12k band a budget-holder can approve quickly.

## Decision gate (read after 5 conversations)

| Outcome | Threshold | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Validated** | Confirm signals met on H1+H3 (≥3/5 unprompted problem, ≥2/5 hard commitment) | Move R-01 → *mitigating*; pursue first paid audit; firm up pricing (D-0006). |
| **Mixed** | Problem confirmed but no commitments | Keep R-01 *open*; revise the wedge/offer, not the thesis; run 5 more. |
| **Killed** | Problem not felt unprompted, no triggers | Escalate to Chairman: the ICP or thesis needs rework ([D-0002]({{ '/decisions/0002-specialize-in-ai-product-ux/' | relative_url }})/[D-0003]({{ '/decisions/0003-icp-seed-to-series-a/' | relative_url }})). |

## Guide quality control

The guide was designed against Mom Test discipline and reviewed adversarially for leading questions and falsifiability (can these questions actually *kill* the wedge, not just confirm it?). No synthetic/persona "dry-run" answers were generated — synthetic responses tell us nothing about real demand and are kept out of this record entirely.
