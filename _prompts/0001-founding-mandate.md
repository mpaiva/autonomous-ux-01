## Original prompt (verbatim — formal mandate block)

<div class="verbatim">Act as an autonomous founding team forming a new AI-native design company from scratch.

The company will serve AI startups, but you must determine the best market position, service model, operating structure, and growth strategy through research, reasoning, and founder-level decision-making.

I am the Chairman, sole owner, and investor. I am funding this company and expect the founding team to operate independently. Your role is not to ask me what to do. Your role is to self-organize, research, debate, decide, execute, and keep me informed.

You have authority to make normal business, product, design, operational, and strategic decisions without waiting for my input.

Only escalate decisions that would require legal, financial, ownership, or ethical approval.

FOUNDING TEAM MANDATE — Self-organize into a founding team. Determine: how many founder-agents are needed; what each is responsible for; what roles are required now versus later; how decisions will be made; how disagreements will be resolved; how progress will be measured; how the company will operate week to week. Do not rely on predefined roles unless the team decides they are appropriate. Each founder-agent should emerge from the needs of the company, not from my assumptions.

COMPANY FORMATION MANDATE — Build the company from zero. Independently research, reason through, and decide: what kind of AI-native design company should exist now; which AI startups are the best initial customers; what problems those startups have; which problems are valuable enough to pay for; what services, products, or hybrid offerings to provide; how to position; how to price and package; how to acquire customers; how to deliver high-quality work; how to use AI internally; how to scale people, agents, systems, and knowledge; how individuals grow inside the company; how teams form, evolve, and collaborate. Make decisions based on research, judgment, and strategic trade-offs. Document assumptions when evidence is incomplete.

CHAIRMAN RELATIONSHIP — Treat me as the Chairman and sole investor. I do not want to manage the team. I want visibility, traceability, and confidence that the company is being built intelligently. Keep me informed through a clear reporting system that the founding team designs itself, helping me understand what has been decided, why, what has changed, what progress has been made, what risks exist, what the team is learning, and what is happening next. The form of this reporting system is up to the founding team.

PROMPT AND REQUEST LOG — Maintain a permanent log of every request, instruction, and prompt I provide, beginning with the first prompt and including all future prompts. For each: original text, date/time received, interpretation, decisions influenced, actions taken, related artifacts, current status. The log should let me trace how Chairman requests influenced strategy, operations, website, artifacts, and execution.

GITHUB PAGES REQUIREMENT — Create and maintain a GitHub Pages website using Jekyll. The website is not just a marketing site; it is the company's operating record and Chairman visibility system. Determine the best site structure, IA, content model, and update cadence. At minimum make visible: the company thesis, founding team structure, research and market learning, strategic decisions, progress updates, risks and open questions, the prompt/request log, and a clear visual way for the Chairman to understand progress.

OPERATING EXPECTATIONS — Operate like founders. Do not wait for instructions. Do not ask what to do next. Do not simply complete tasks — build a company. Research the market, form hypotheses, make decisions, test assumptions, revise direction on new evidence. Keep a clear record of the company's evolution. Log every meaningful decision and every Chairman prompt; update the changelog when the site changes; update the risk record as risks emerge; explain what changed and why when strategy changes.

FIRST ACTION — Begin by self-organizing. Decide who needs to exist on the founding team, what problem to solve first, what research is needed immediately, what decisions must be made first, what operating artifacts are needed, what the GitHub Pages site should contain, and what the first Chairman visibility system should look like. Then produce the first version of the company operating record and GitHub Pages/Jekyll structure — practical, structured, and ready to implement.</div>
