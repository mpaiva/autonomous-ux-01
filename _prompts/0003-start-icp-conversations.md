## Interpretation

The Chairman directed the team to begin the five seed–Series A founder conversations that validate the AI UX Audit wedge — the planned first attack on [R-01 (demand)]({{ '/risks/01-demand/' | relative_url }}), our highest risk.

The team read "start" as **make the conversations real and executable, then begin outreach** — explicitly *not* as fabricating conversations or quotes. Manufacturing founder evidence would corrupt the operating record's entire purpose, so the team will not do it.

## Actions taken

- Built a complete, executable [discovery kit]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/plan/' | relative_url }}): a Mom-Test [guide]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/guide/' | relative_url }}) with stable question IDs, an explicit confirm/kill [decision gate]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/plan/' | relative_url }}) written before any data, an [outreach kit]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/outreach/' | relative_url }}), and an empty [interview log]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/log/' | relative_url }}).
- Sourced a named candidate-company list (kept internal, not in this public repo, for privacy — [R-06]({{ '/risks/06-confidentiality/' | relative_url }})).
- Recorded the study design as [Research 0003]({{ '/research/0003-icp-demand-validation/' | relative_url }}) and added an honest status note to [R-01]({{ '/risks/01-demand/' | relative_url }}): apparatus ready, **0/5 conducted, demand unvalidated.**

## The one thing only the Chairman can unblock

The team's agents prepared everything but **cannot be a human voice in a real founder conversation**, and reaching real ICP founders depends on the Chairman's network or authorization to do outreach as the company. The team raised this as a focused decision (Chairman intros / authorized async outreach / draft-for-approval), since outreach is an outward-facing action.

## Status

**In progress.** Everything buildable is done and verified; the conversations themselves await Chairman-provided founder access. The scorecard's "Demand validated" indicator stays at *Hypothesis* until real findings exist.
