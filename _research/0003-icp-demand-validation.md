> **This is a study *design*, not a result.** It deliberately carries no findings. Building the kit does **not** reduce [R-01]({{ '/risks/01-demand/' | relative_url }}); demand is validated only after real conversations meet the [decision gate]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/plan/' | relative_url }}).

## Why

[R-01 — will AI startups actually pay for design?]({{ '/risks/01-demand/' | relative_url }}) is the one risk that can kill the company, and pricing ([D-0006]({{ '/decisions/0006-pricing-hypothesis/' | relative_url }})) is still `proposed`. Five structured founder conversations are the cheapest way to attack both.

## What we built

A complete, executable discovery kit:

- **[Discovery guide]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/guide/' | relative_url }})** — a Mom-Test interview script with stable question IDs, designed to surface real pain and willingness-to-pay *without pitching or anchoring*, and to be able to **kill** the wedge, not just confirm it.
- **[Sprint plan]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/plan/' | relative_url }})** — hypotheses (H1–H4) and an explicit confirm-vs-kill decision gate written *before* any data.
- **[Outreach kit]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/outreach/' | relative_url }})** — ICP screen, sourcing channels, and recruiting message templates.
- **[Interview log]({{ '/research/icp-demand-validation/log/' | relative_url }})** — capture template, currently empty (0/5).
- A **named candidate-company list**, kept internal (not this public repo) for privacy.

## The honest constraint

The founding team's agents prepared the guide and will synthesize results, but **a human must run the actual conversations** — and reaching real ICP founders needs the Chairman's intros or authorization to do outreach. That is the one open dependency, raised in [P-0003]({{ '/log/0003-start-icp-conversations/' | relative_url }}). No conversation will be fabricated to fill the gap.

## Next

Secure founder access → run 5 → capture in the log → synthesize into a results entry → update R-01 and the scorecard per the gate.
