## 1. Productized "design subscription" (Design-as-a-Service)

Flat monthly fee, queue-based, pause anytime — now a mature category.

- **DesignJoy** — ~$4,995/mo (from $5,995), one request at a time, ~48hr turnaround. ([designjoy.co](https://www.designjoy.co/))
- **Superside** — roughly $5,000–$45,000/mo, ~1-year contract + ~$1,000/mo platform fee. ([superside.com](https://www.superside.com/blog/unlimited-design-services), [vendr.com](https://www.vendr.com/marketplace/superside))
- Entry graphic subscriptions ~$400–$600/mo; comprehensive packages $4,000–$8,000+/mo. ([orbix.studio](https://www.orbix.studio/blogs/ux-ui-design-cost-guide-2025))

Most subscription brands skew to marketing/graphic/Webflow output, **not** deep product/UX strategy.

## 2. Retainer & project pricing (product/UX agencies for tech startups)

- **Retainers:** commonly $3,000–$20,000/mo; SaaS w/ strategy ~$7,000–$12,000/mo mid-market; embedded teams $12,000–$30,000+/mo. ([dribbble.com](https://dribbble.com/resources/tips/ui-ux-design-agency-costs), [925studios.co](https://www.925studios.co/blog/how-much-does-a-ux-agency-cost))
- **Projects:** sprints from ~$5,000; typical $15,000–$50,000; full redesigns $100,000–$150,000+. ([foundey.com](https://foundey.com/blog/how-much-do-agencies-charge-for-ui-ux-design))
- **Rates:** US senior agencies $100–$250/hr. Retainers often 3–6 month minimums. ([procreator.design](https://procreator.design/blog/how-much-does-a-ui-ux-design-agency-cost/), [atico3.com](https://atico3.com/en/blog/design-subscription-vs-agency-retainer/))

## 3. Embedded / fractional design lead

Less standardized. Fractional execs broadly $5,000–$10,000/mo per client for ~10–40 hrs/mo. Specialist firms (Outwitly, Frilled, Common Ideas) exist but **publish no fixed price** — a genuine evidence gap. ([saasconsult.co](https://saasconsult.co/blog/fractional-cmo-pricing/), [outwitly.com](https://outwitly.com/services/fractional-design-leadership/))

## 4. Emerging AI-native studios

The category is real as of 2026, mostly "contact us" pricing.

- **Adam Fard UX Studio** — explicitly positions on LLM products and agent UX. ([adamfard.com](https://adamfard.com/))
- **925Studios** — seed-to-Series-B SaaS/AI, premium, strategy-led. ([925studios.co](https://www.925studios.co/))
- **Punchcut, Clay, Lazarev.agency** — cited leading AI product studios. ([punchcut.com](https://punchcut.com/perspectives/best-ai-design-agencies/), [lazarev.agency](https://www.lazarev.agency/articles/ai-product-design-agencies))
- Studios built specifically around **eval tooling / agent-observability UX** — **not found**. Open white-space.

## 5. Equity-for-design / hybrid

Real but niche and bespoke. Hybrid cash + equity; stakes typically **1–8%** (Gin Lane ~1–3% while requiring cash to cover opex). ([medium.com](https://medium.com/@robjbye/the-designers-guide-to-startup-equity-86351062700e), [inc.com](https://www.inc.com/magazine/201510/graham-winfrey/do-you-dare-to-take-shares.html))

## Implications for Throughline

- **Anchor a productized tier** in the proven $5–8k/mo band but differentiate on product/UX + AI depth, not throughput.
- **Embedded "fractional AI design lead" ~$10–18k/mo** — above generic fractional, justified by scarce expertise and the absence of public competitors there.
- **Project pricing $20–60k**, full builds $100k+.
- **White-space to own:** eval/observability + agent-interaction UX — no named competitor.
- **Equity:** stay cash-positive; 1–3% as selective upside.

**Evidence gaps:** fractional-design-lead price cards, AI-studio public pricing, and eval-focused studios are not publicly documented; those figures are analogues or absences, not confirmed rates.
